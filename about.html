About Us

The Kopaline University I.T Student Association is a student-led organization dedicated to fostering a community of tech enthusiasts. We aim to provide a platform for students to learn, collaborate, and innovate in the field of Information Technology.

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the tech industry through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects.
